# The Living Lab: Technical Research Ecosystem

## Overview
This repository serves as the centralized **Professional Landing Page** and **Research Hub** for **The Living Lab**. Grounded in **nearly 30 years of lived experience** navigating federal housing systems, this ecosystem transitions longitudinal ethnographic data into scalable **Human Performance Technology (HPT)** interventions.

## Professional Identity
* **Lead Researcher:** Joana Camp 
* **Academic Status:** Senior, Old Dominion University (3.95 GPA) 
* **Target Industry:** Training Specialist / Behavioral Design & Public Policy 

### Core Research Objectives
* **Mitigate the "Bandwidth Tax":** Reducing the 30% cognitive drain caused by administrative friction in federal programs.
* **Linguistic Equity:** Implementing bilingual (Heritage Spanish/English) logic checks to eliminate the "Signature Gap".
* **Scalable Compliance:** Developing AI-driven tools to assist vulnerable populations in maintaining regulatory compliance.

## Methodology & Theoretical Framework
The design of this hub and its linked prototypes are guided by:
* **Mayer’s Cognitive Theory of Multimedia Learning:** Utilizing Spatial Contiguity to reduce the "Split-Attention Effect".
* **Human Performance Technology (HPT):** Systematic investigation of performance gaps and instructional interventions.
* **Ethnographic Auditing:** Leveraging lived experience as primary data for technical requirements.
